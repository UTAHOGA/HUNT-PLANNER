The Research Database We Need

This page is the home for the Utah hunt research database. It should help a visitor move from "what hunts exist" to "which hunt is actually right for me" using official Utah DWR data, deadlines, and practical hunt-quality signals.

Next build targets: permit totals, success rates, harvest statistics, draw odds, regulation links, application deadlines, and a hunt-by-hunt calendar.

Why This Matters

Hunters need more than a map. They need a decision engine: what the hunt is, how hard it is to draw, how it has performed, and whether it fits their goals.

Data Standard

This research page should stay tied to official Utah DWR source material wherever possible, then add U.O.G.A. interpretation and outfitter context on top of that base.
